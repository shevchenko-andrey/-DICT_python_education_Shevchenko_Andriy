Twitter’s account-verification service lacks a specific category for scientists (see go.nature.com/3mnue4). In my view, this oversight should be promptly rectified, given researchers’ importance for disseminating accurate knowledge with relevant caveats. The platform would then benefit from offering the public reliable scientific points of reference.
